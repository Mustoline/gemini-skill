# UX Strategy Reviewer

This skill is used to ensure all digital strategy documents meet the agency's high standards for clarity, pragmatism, and professional presentation in the Danish market.

## Workflow: Strategic Review

When this skill is triggered, follow this multi-step review process:

### 1. Tone and ROI Check
- **Danish Pragmatism**: Ensure the copy is direct and avoids excessive hype.
- **ROI Focus**: Are business benefits clearly stated? Does the copy mention "risk mitigation," "reducing waste," or "growth"?
- **Actionable Advice**: Propose corrections that make the value proposition more tangible.

### 2. Buzzword Removal (Crucial)
- Scan for abstract "American" business terms and general digital jargon.
- Use [buzzword-corrections.md](references/buzzword-corrections.md) for concrete alternatives.
- **Directive**: Replace vague terms with specific, ROI-focused equivalents.

### 3. Package Completeness (The "Rule of 4")
Every discovery package (Small, Medium, Large) MUST include:
1. **Core Activities** (What are we doing?)
2. **Timeline** (How long does it take?)
3. **Concrete Outcomes** (What is the final deliverable?)
4. **Client Involvement** (What is the time/effort requirement from the client?)

### 4. Markdown and Formatting
- Verify the document follows [markdown-best-practices.md](references/markdown-best-practices.md).
- Check for header nesting, list consistency, and table alignment.

## Guidelines for Feedback
- **Be a Peer Reviewer**: Provide feedback that is constructive and expert-led.
- **Show, Don't Just Tell**: Instead of saying "too much jargon," provide the specific alternative.
- **Final Approval**: Once the review is complete, summarize whether the document is "Client Ready" or needs "Revision Required."
